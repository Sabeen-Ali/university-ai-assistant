Punjab University Admission Policy
University of the Punjab is located in Lahore, Pakistan.
Undergraduate admissions open in July and August every year.
Eligibility for BS programs: Minimum 60% marks in FSc, FA, or equivalent.
Entry test is conducted by Punjab University or NTS.
Required documents: Matric certificate, FSc certificate, CNIC copy, domicile, 4 passport photos.
Merit list is announced on Punjab University official website.
Apply online at: https://admissions.pu.edu.pk
Late applications are not accepted after the deadline.
International students must contact the foreign students office.
