Punjab University Courses and Departments
Punjab University has more than 12 faculties and 70 departments.
Faculty of Arts includes departments of Urdu, English, History, Philosophy.
Faculty of Science includes departments of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology.
Faculty of Commerce includes BS Commerce and MBA programs.
Faculty of Information Technology includes BS Computer Science and BS IT programs.
BS programs are 4 years, 8 semesters, 130 to 136 credit hours.
MS programs are 2 years, 4 semesters, 30 to 36 credit hours.
PhD programs are 3 years minimum.
Course registration is done online through Punjab University student portal.
Maximum 6 courses per semester are allowed for regular students.
Students must maintain minimum CGPA of 2.0 to continue enrollment.
