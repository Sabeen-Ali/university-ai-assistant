Punjab University General Information
University of the Punjab was established in 1882.
It is the oldest and largest university in Pakistan.
Punjab University is located in Lahore, Pakistan.
Main campus address: Canal Bank Road, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan.
Vice Chancellor office is at Quaid-e-Azam campus.
Punjab University has more than 35,000 students enrolled.
Contact number: 042-99231103
Official website: www.pu.edu.pk
Admissions website: admissions.pu.edu.pk
Results website: results.pu.edu.pk
Punjab University offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs.
Distance education programs are also available through PUCIT and other departments.
