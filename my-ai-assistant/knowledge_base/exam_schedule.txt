Punjab University Exam Schedule and Policies
Punjab University Controller of Examinations manages all exams.
Annual examination system is followed in many departments.
Semester system is followed in IT, Commerce, and Science faculties.
Mid semester exams are held in week 8 of each semester.
Final exams are held at end of semester, usually in January and June.
Students must have 75% attendance to appear in exams.
Admit card is required to enter examination hall.
No mobile phones allowed in examination hall.
Result is announced on Punjab University website at results.pu.edu.pk
Students can apply for rechecking within 30 days of result announcement.
Rechecking fee is 1000 PKR per subject.
Degrees are issued from Quaid-e-Azam campus Lahore.
