Punjab University Hostel and Transport
Punjab University has separate hostels for male and female students.
Female hostels are located inside the main campus for security.
Male hostels are available near the campus.
Hostel fee is approximately 5,000 to 8,000 PKR per month including meals.
Hostel admission is given to outstation students on priority basis.
Apply for hostel at the time of university admission.
Punjab University campus is located on Canal Bank Road, Lahore.
University bus service is available from different areas of Lahore.
Transport fee is approximately 2,000 to 4,000 PKR per month.
New Campus and Old Campus are connected by university shuttle service.
Library is the Allama Iqbal Library, one of the largest in Pakistan.
Library timings are Monday to Saturday 8 AM to 8 PM.
