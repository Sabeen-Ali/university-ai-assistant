Punjab University Fee Structure
BS Programs fee is approximately 30,000 to 50,000 PKR per semester depending on department.
MS Programs fee is approximately 45,000 to 70,000 PKR per semester.
PhD Programs fee is approximately 60,000 to 90,000 PKR per semester.
Fee is paid via bank challan at HBL or UBL bank branches.
Fee can also be paid online through Punjab University portal.
Late fee fine is charged after due date.
Need-based scholarships are available for deserving students.
Merit scholarships available for students with GPA above 3.5.
HEC scholarships are also available for Punjab University students.
Fee concession available for hafiz-e-quran students.
